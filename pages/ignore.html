Setup Procedure

1. To exclude certain parts of a page

To prevent WOVN.io from extracting specific parts of a web page, in your HTML code, enclose those specific parts with “wovn-ignore“.

As an example, to exclude “Hello” from extraction:

<div wovn-ignore>
  Hello
</div>

To exclude “GoodEvening”:

<div>Me should not be translated
  <div>
    Good Evening
   </div>
</div>

2. To ignore by class

<div>

<a class=”after-login-id”> Me should not be translated </a>

</ div>

3. To ignore by selector

<div>

<a class=”after-login-id”> Me should not be translated </a>

</ div>

In this case, that page gets removed from the WOVN.io Dashboard.

3. To exclude on multiple pages or to exclude without modifying your HTML

If you would need to exclude the same class on multiple pages or you do not want to modify your HTML, you can add a specific class name to your project’s Settings.
